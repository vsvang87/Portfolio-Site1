About Me

My journey into tech was an unexpected one. I came across code by accident. I worked as a CNC Machinist and wanted to advance my career by learning more G & M code. When I google for CNC codes, software programming pop up as well. Out of curiosity I decided to dive in and check it out.

Just from the look of code it was intimidating. I thought you would have to go to college and be smart to learn. After doing research it was clear that anyone could make it as long as you are willing to put in the work and grind. I found many good online courses and videos on youtube. I decided to take the frontend developer path because I'm a visual person.
